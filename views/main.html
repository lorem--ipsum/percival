What's that ?

Percival is an AngularJS directive that offers an user-friendly editor for logical expressions. It reads from and writes to an Abstract Syntax Tree, nothing less, nothing more.

How can I use it ?

Look below : on the left, there is the directive in action. Add, remove, edit whatever you want, and look at the right. There is the model, updated in real time thanks to the AngularJS magic.
